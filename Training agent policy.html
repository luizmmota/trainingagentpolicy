01 — Overview

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and connect it to third-party services such as the WHOOP platform.

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Plain language summary: We only collect what we need, we never sell your data, and you can delete your information at any time.
02 — Data Collected

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — name, email address, and profile details you provide when creating an account.
  • Health & fitness data — data retrieved via the WHOOP API with your explicit authorization, including recovery scores, sleep data, strain metrics, heart rate, and workout summaries.
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with the application, such as features used, timestamps, and session duration.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers for security and analytics purposes.
  • OAuth tokens — access and refresh tokens issued by WHOOP, stored securely to maintain your connection.

We do not collect data you have not authorized, and we do not access your WHOOP account beyond the scopes you explicitly approve during the OAuth flow.

03 — Use of Data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the application and its features.
  • Display your WHOOP health and fitness data within the application.
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain secure sessions.
  • Send you service-related communications (e.g., account notices, updates).
  • Analyze aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to improve the product.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We will never use your health data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not described in this policy without your explicit consent.

04 — Data Sharing

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who assist in operating our service (e.g., cloud hosting, error monitoring), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred, with notice provided to you.

All third parties we work with are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

05 — WHOOP Integration

WHOOP API & Health Data

Our application integrates with the WHOOP Developer API. When you connect your WHOOP account, you authorize our application to access specific data scopes on your behalf.

  • Access is granted through WHOOP's official OAuth 2.0 authorization flow — we never see your WHOOP password.
  • We only request the minimum data scopes required to provide the features you use.
  • You may revoke our application's access to your WHOOP data at any time through your WHOOP account settings.
  • Revoking access will stop any future data retrieval; previously retrieved data will be deleted upon your request.
Your health data is treated with the highest level of sensitivity. It is never shared with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

This application is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WHOOP. Use of WHOOP data is subject to WHOOP's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

06 — Security

Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest (including OAuth tokens).
  • Access controls limiting which personnel can access user data.
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

07 — Your Rights

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days.
